Local-first personal backup

Keep your own Foursquare check-ins.

Quadratic is a local command-line app for backing up your own Foursquare and Swarm check-in history. It runs on your machine, stores data on your machine, and exists to help you keep your own archive.

Read privacy policy Source repository

Direct to your device

The app authenticates the user and downloads backup data directly to local storage.

No hosted data sink

This website does not receive or store check-in history. It exists for product information and policy disclosure only.

Personal use

Quadratic is intended for users exporting and preserving their own check-in history for personal backup purposes.

How it works

  • You authorize Quadratic with your Foursquare account.
  • The local app exchanges that authorization for an access token.
  • The local app downloads your check-in history to files on your computer.
  • You control the resulting backup and can remove it at any time.

Run It On a Mac

Quadratic is a command-line app. To run it on macOS:

  • Install Go.
  • Clone dorkitude/quadratic.
  • Build it with go build -o quadratic ..
  • Run ./quadratic init, then ./quadratic login, ./quadratic sync, and ./quadratic browse.
