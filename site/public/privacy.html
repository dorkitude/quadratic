Quadratic Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

Quadratic is a local-first tool for backing up a user's own Foursquare and Swarm check-in data.

What Quadratic does

Quadratic runs locally on the user's device. After the user authorizes the application with Foursquare, the local app downloads check-in data directly to the user's machine for personal backup purposes.

What data we collect

We do not collect, receive, host, or store your check-in history on our servers through this website.

The website associated with Quadratic exists only to provide basic product information and this privacy policy.

How data is handled

  • Your Foursquare check-in data is downloaded by the local app to your device.
  • Your backup files are stored locally under paths you control.
  • We do not sell, share, inspect, analyze, or monetize your check-in history.

OAuth authorization

Quadratic uses Foursquare's OAuth flow so that a user can authorize access to their own account data. That authorization is used by the local app to request the user's own data from Foursquare.

Data deletion

Because backup data is stored locally by the user, deletion is under the user's control. Users may delete the local backup files at any time and may revoke the application's access through their Foursquare account settings.

Contact

For questions, bug reports, or policy concerns, please file an issue at github.com/dorkitude/quadratic/issues.
